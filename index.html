IEEE EDS NSEC
Student Branch Chapter

IEEE EDS SBC believes in education

We are one of the most active Student Branch Chapter in our Kolkata Section belonging to IEEE region 10. We aim to promote Electronics Engineering and have talented individuals to do so.

We build.
We Innovate.
We Create.

Seminars and Events

We conduct one-day seminars on Electronic Devices related topics. We invite speakers from across the globe. They are can be IEEE Distinguished Lecturers or Dean of some university or our alumni.

Projects

Our student members are involved in a multitude of projects. These inhouse project development help students to build experience and a portfolio. You can look out for our GitHub page.

Classes

We believe in organizing classes for the students by the students. The students get an opportunity to share what they have learned from the events we organize or by themselves.

Humanitarian Activities

We help high school students by educating them about Electronics and why one can opt for Electronics Engineering.

What We do

Our primary aim is to promote the advancements in the field of Electronic Solid State Devices. We do so by organizing seminars and talks. We invite IEEE Distinguished Lecturers and other prominent speakers. We don't shy away from giving our student members a platform to share their knowledge. We go to high schools to teach the fundamentals of electronics and guide them in choosing a career in engineering. Our students also work on exciting projects. The self-balancing robot, smart attendance system are some of the projects we have worked on.
